How to Find Your Becker Serial Number

Model Number Example Serials

Example: "BE2580 15093945"

To locate your Becker serial number remove the radio from the dashboard. The serial and model number is located on the radio label on the top or side of the radio.

The serial will be a number or a letter and a number the model number starts with "BE", in some cases it will be engraved into the radios casing.
