How to Find Audi Chorus Radio Serial No.

1. How To Find Your Audi Chorus Radio Serial.

There are 2 types of Audi Chorus serial numbers (Audi Chorus Navigation Plus see method 2). It is quite simple to remove your Audi Chorus radio. You will need a set of radio release keys (PC5-83). These are inexpensive tools, you can obtain them from Amazon.

Once you have the radio release keys, push them into the 4 holes in each corner of the radio (See image), apply a little pressure and the radio will release from the dashboard. The radios label will be located on the radio's casing.

2. What To Do Next

The Audi radio serial is a 14 digit serial number for example AUZ1Z1F6412082. Please see image for reference.
