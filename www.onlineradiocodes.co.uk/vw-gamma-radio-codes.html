How To Get My VW Gamma Code

Removing Your Gamma Radio

Removing your Gamma radio requires a screwdriver & prying tool. remove the plastic trim from the dash unscrew all four holding screws.

Older radios are taken out using radio release keys, The keys are pressed into each corner of the radio & free the unit from the dash.

There are guides & video tutorials on You Tube this will help if you are struggling taking out the unit.

Volkswagen Gamma Label Example
  • There are different types of Gamma radio labels, sometimes identifying the correct information can cause issues.
  • All VW serials start "VWZ", an example is "VWZ1Z2H1234567". Ensure the serial is correct to get the correct decode for your radios unit.
