How To Find A S-Max B Max Serial Number?

Ford 6000CD

Start by holding down pre-set buttons 1 & 6 together on your Ford stereo.
6000CD Radios will begin to cycle through different information on-screen.

Your serial will begin with “V” followed by six digits, the example serial number is “V197905”

If the serial did not display, try buttons 2 & 6, a serial number beginning with “M” followed by 6 numbers will appear on-screen.

Ford TravelPilot

Ford Travel pilot radios the serial number can only be obtained from the back of the unit, as shown in the picture, there are small coin shaped hole in each corner of the radio.

Once the radio is removed the serial number is printed on the label on the back of the radio, please use the image for reference in finding your serial number.
